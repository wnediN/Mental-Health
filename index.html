Supporting Mental Health in Athletes

Athletes are strong — but mental health matters just as much as physical strength. Let's break the stigma and build a community that lifts each other up.

Explore Coping Strategies

Welcome!

Mind Over Muscle is a student-led initiative to raise awareness about mental health challenges faced by athletes. Whether you're dealing with pressure, anxiety, burnout, or just need someone to talk to — you're not alone.

As an artistic swimmer, I’ve experienced the pressures firsthand. Through this project, I hope to create a space where we can openly talk about these challenges and find ways to support each other.
