About This Project

Mind Over Muscle is a student-led initiative to raise awareness about mental health challenges faced by athletes. Whether you're dealing with pressure, anxiety, burnout, or just need someone to talk to — you're not alone.

As an artistic swimmer, I have faced moments of extreme mental and physical fatigue. The beauty and demands of the sport taught me resilience but also showed me the importance of taking care of my mental health. I hope to inspire others to prioritize their mental wellbeing alongside their athletic goals.

Scientific Research

Studies show that mental health challenges like anxiety, depression, and burnout are significantly prevalent among competitive athletes. According to a 2019 study published in the British Journal of Sports Medicine, nearly 35% of elite athletes suffer from a mental health crisis at some point in their careers.

Research also emphasizes the importance of resilience training, psychological support, and reducing stigma in athletic environments to protect and enhance performance and overall well-being.
