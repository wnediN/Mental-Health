Mindfulness Techniques

Improve your focus, awareness, and calmness with simple exercises you can practice daily.

Breathing Exercises

Slow, deep breaths help lower heart rate and control performance anxiety. Try inhaling for 4 seconds, holding for 7 seconds, and exhaling for 8 seconds.

Body Scans

Progressively focus on each part of your body, from toes to head, noticing tension and releasing it.

Gratitude Journaling

Writing down 3 things you are thankful for each day can shift your mindset and improve emotional resilience.
